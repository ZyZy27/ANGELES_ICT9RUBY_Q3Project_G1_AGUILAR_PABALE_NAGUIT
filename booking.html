Manila Light Rail Transit

The Manila Metro Rail Transit (MRT) and the Manila Light Rail Transit System (MLT) is a rapid transit systems in Manila, Philippines. MRT consists of one line and 13 stations, and MLT consists of two lines and 31 stations. MRT began operation in December 1999, and MLT in December 1984.

LRT runs from 05:00 to 22:00. On weekends, service ends at 21:00 - 21:30.
