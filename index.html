How to Book a Ride

1

Register for a LuzonLink Rail account to access our booking services.

2

Select your departure and destination stations using our Fare Matrix.

3

Confirm your ride and get ready to experience fast and convenient rail travel.

Passenger Tips & Reminders

1

Arrive at the station at least 15 minutes before your scheduled departure.

2

Keep your ticket or QR code handy for quick boarding.

3

Follow all safety signs and instructions while on thr platform and inside the train.

4

Respect fellow passengers and maintain a clean environment on board.
